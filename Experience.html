I started selling scarves in 2021 to get used to running a small business such as tudung bawal (b45) and shawls. Alhamdulillah, the beginning of the business was positively greeted by clients and friends from instagram (@tieykascarvf & @tieykajamal). It stems from a desire to learn and experience how to earn money while remaining thrifty and not overly opulent because obtaining the worth of RM1 is not easy. However, I only sell non-continuous items and am only active in business during the semester break so that I can concentrate on my schoolwork. This is because, despite coming from a business family (my mother is a trader), I want to gain business experience. A mother's interest prompted me to give it a shot.
